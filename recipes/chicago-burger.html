The Chicago Burger

A delisious pickle-bun-burger from the oldtown Chicago

The preffered meal of the nutorious chicago warriors in the elder 18 hundres.

Includes the finest pickles from the almightry garden in downtown

Ingredients

  • 10 pickles
  • 1 bun
  • patty
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Cut the pickles into small pieces
  2. heat up the patty on the stove
  3. toast the bun
  4. Cut the bun
  5. put patty in bun
  6. Cover with pickles and ketchup
